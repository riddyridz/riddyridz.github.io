✎about

Hi, I'm Ridhwan. I build and maintain software, ranging from small automation scripts to full applications. This little site is my notebook: a page that starts out nearly blank and fills up as more gets shipped.

I like the part where a messy problem turns into a clean line of code, and the quiet satisfaction of a script that just runs. Most of what I make lives over on GitHub; the notes about it end up here.
